About AFD

Artivism for Democracy (AFD) uses art, storytelling and digital media to inspire reflection and dialogue on democratic values. Through residencies, public showcases and online curation we amplify youth and women-led voices across Africa.

  • Duration: 10 months (residency & showcase)
  • Participants: 15 emerging artivists
  • Primary location: Addis Ababa + online

Why it matters

Culture shapes how people imagine participation and rights. AFD centers creative practice as a way to deepen civic imagination, open dialogue, and document democratic experiences that are often left out of mainstream discourse.

What We Do

Residencies & Workshops

Intensive mentorship and co-creation sessions where artists learn civic research methods, collaborative production, and digital storytelling techniques.

Exhibitions & Digital Showcases

Hybrid public exhibits and an online gallery to share and document works produced during the program.

Democracy Dialogues

Public conversations that bring together artists, civic actors, and communities to reflect on the works and themes produced in the residency.

Community Storytelling

Participatory projects that document local democratic experiences through photography, audio, and performance.
